Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about iOTA Math Academy.

Which classes does iOTA Math Academy teach? ▼
iOTA Math Academy provides Mathematics coaching for students of Classes 6, 7, 8, 9, and 10. Our teaching covers the school syllabus comprehensively for all these grades.
Are online classes available? ▼
Yes! We offer both online and offline classes. Online sessions are conducted as live, interactive classes — not pre-recorded videos — so students get the same quality of teaching and can ask questions in real time.
Where is iOTA Math Academy located? ▼
iOTA Math Academy is based in Pune, Maharashtra. Offline students can attend classes at our Pune centre, while students from outside Pune can join our online batches.
Is there a demo class available before enrolling? ▼
Absolutely! We offer a free demo class so students and parents can experience Poonam Ma'am's teaching style and assess if it's the right fit — with zero pressure or commitment.
What is the difference between the Basic and Advanced levels? ▼
The Basic level is designed for students who want to strengthen their Maths foundations, clear concepts, and gain confidence. The Advanced level is for students who already have a good grasp and want to tackle challenging problems, score higher marks, and prepare for board exams. We assess each student before placing them in the right batch.
How can I contact iOTA Math Academy? ▼
You can reach us by phone at 9222202029, by email at iotaedtech@gmail.com, or by filling in the contact form on this page. We respond promptly to all enquiries.
Are study materials provided? ▼
Yes, students receive notes, worksheets, and practice questions as part of their learning programme. These materials are designed to supplement class teaching and support regular revision at home.
How are batches organised? ▼
Batches are kept intentionally small to ensure every student receives adequate individual attention. Students are grouped by class and level so the teaching pace suits everyone in the batch.
